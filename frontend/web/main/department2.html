Newport Police Department News

2017-07-03T17:32:09+0000

On July 4th, the annual fireworks display is scheduled to commence at 9:15 p.m. from Fort Adams. Public parking is available at the Gateway Center on America’s Cup and at the Waterfront Parking Lot on Mary Street.

As in the past years, we are anticipating heavy traffic so plan your travels accordingly.

There will be uniformed Newport Police Officers to assist with traffic control. Please be mindful of the no parking areas in the city.

We hope everyone has a safe and healthy 4th of July.

2017-06-30T23:43:29+0000

The Newport Police Department will be partnering with the United States Coast Guard over the Holiday weekend on Operation Dry Water. The mission is to reduce the number of alcohol- and drug-related accidents and fatalities through increased recreational boater awareness and by fostering a stronger and more visible deterrent to alcohol use on the water.
